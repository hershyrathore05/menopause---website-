Stay Active

Regular exercise helps manage weight, reduce hot flashes, improve mood, and strengthen bones:

  • Aerobic exercises: walking, swimming, cycling (30 min daily)
  • Strength training: bodyweight exercises, resistance bands
  • Flexibility & balance: yoga, stretching, Pilates

Start gradually and consult your doctor if needed.
