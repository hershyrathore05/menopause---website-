Healthy Eating

During menopause, women should focus on maintaining bone health, regulating weight, and reducing inflammation. Include:

  • Calcium-rich foods: milk, yogurt, cheese, leafy greens
  • Vitamin D sources: salmon, eggs, fortified foods
  • High-fiber foods: whole grains, fruits, vegetables
  • Antioxidant-rich foods: berries, nuts, green tea

Avoid processed foods and excessive sugar, and drink plenty of water daily.
