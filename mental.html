Understanding Mental Changes

Women may experience mood swings, irritability, anxiety, low energy, and sleep disturbances. Awareness and self-care are essential.

Tips for a Smooth Transition

  • Practice mindfulness, meditation, or yoga daily.
  • Maintain a strong support network of family and friends.
  • Stay physically active to improve mood.
  • Keep a journal to track emotions and triggers.
  • Seek professional counseling if needed.

Positive Mindset

View menopause as a natural life stage, not a burden. Focus on routines that support mental and physical wellness to reduce stress.
